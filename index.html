ABOUT US

Quiet Cup was founded in 2018 by Southville native Melissa Jones. While away at college Melissa loved to work in coffee shops, and found her hometown was lacking in spaces like these. She founded Quiet Cup to provide locals with a space outside of the house or office to welcome productivity and focus, and encourage motivation.

Quiet Cup offers a several spacious work areas to choose from equipped with fast internet, outlets, and a free charging station. The several monthly events that they participate in encourages students and workers to engage in their community while also providing them educational and career opportunities.
